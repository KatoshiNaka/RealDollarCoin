ABOUT $RUSD

Real Dollar is a community driven token that is currently hosted on BNB Smart Chain, that aims to become the medium of exchange between all crypto currencies in the near future. As the Crypto industry strides towards becoming the global financial system, the sheer number of different cryptocurrencies poses an issue that needs to be resolved. There needs to be a medium of exchange between cryptocurrencies that could help everyone in the community determine the value of every coin.

Currently the US Dollar forms the standard at which all cryptocurrencies are valued. However, one of the many issues with the US Dollar and every other Fiat currency is that they are not crypto. Therefore, the network for Fiat Currencies are managed by untrustful entities, ‘The Banks’ and Government. These entities are now coming up with a solution for the industry that are CBDCS, which are essentially the same currencies, but they are just digital. Which basically gives them more control and oversight on every party that is going to deal in crypto, which is completely opposite of what SATOSHI NAKAMOTO set out to do with the very first Crypto, Bitcoin.

Real Dollar, will compete directly with CBDCs to become the future medium of exchange of cryptocurrencies that is transparent yet private, uncontrolled by any singular entity, and will have a value that will be constantly decided by the community.
